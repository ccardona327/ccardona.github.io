Tools for Success

In order to succeed in mathematics it is important to be aware of the resources available and be prepared for class with the necessary tools for learning and a positibe attitude. In addition, students should seek help if needed, attend study sessions or ask questions that will enhace ones learning.

There are different places around campus where studetns can get help with mathematics. Students should not hessitate to seek help, and the sooner students create a routine of attending this places and asking for help, even when the students think they do not need help it is recommended to make use of this designated studying areas.
The Math Assistance Complex (MAC) is a place where students can seek help in mathematics from almost any level.
An additional place where students could get help in a different set up is the MILE located at the Urban Mile building in room 301. There one can make use of their graphing calculators and learn how to use such calculators if you do not know how.
